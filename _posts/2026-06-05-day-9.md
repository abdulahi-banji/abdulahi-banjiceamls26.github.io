  Today's session started with Daniel walking us through Computer Vision. We revised some terms and terminologies in AI & ML. We had lots of hands-on experience building with OpenCV and YOLO in Google Colab. After that we went into our breakout rooms with our team members to prepare for our presentations, assigned slides, and practiced. Preparing the slides was really chill, and running through the presentation gave us a feel for what it will be like presenting our findings.
